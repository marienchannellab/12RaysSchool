Who we are?

Our school teaches a form of ancient Chi Gong, that has been adapted and combined with certain principles of ancient Yoga and advanced breathing techniques, in order to meet the requirements of physiological and mental natures of the modern human.

We do follow the practices of flawlessness and self-attainment, whereby the same routine (Chi Gong routine that we practice does change slightly over time, however it’s structure remains the same) is practiced for a long period of time, because we believe, that practicing the same routine can unlock:

  • a) the routine’s true potential as a pathway to spiritual progress;
  • b) it helps build up a neophyte’s energy potential, cleanse energy channels that run through energy bodies;
  • c) as a result, lead to substantial progress.

Think of a university degree, where you delve deeper into a subject, gaining a greater more detailed insight, yet you still retain the grand picture.

Similarly, when one practices our routine, one does not only improve health, but develop prior qualities necessary to attain higher states of consciousness – namely: patience, endurance, humbleness, ego control, self-motivation, responsibility for one’s thoughts and actions, modesty, ability to keep one’s achievements quietly to one’s self among many others.The latter, being the most valuable of all.

Our school encourages only one form of competition, competition against your old yesterday’s self. If you can regularly come to the class, in spite of troubles at home, at work, health issues, weather conditions, your mood, you will be greatly rewarded, and the rewards will come in different shapes and forms including but notwithstanding the most valuable form of all – energy gain.

What it is, and how to achieve such a gain, we invite you to find out by attending our classes. Last, but not least, only you are responsible for your own spiritual growth, for your ups and downs, and only you can be held accountable for your failure to realize your potential and it is up to the individual spirit, person, human, to ensure that a lifetime of potential growth is not lost in vain. These are the views held by our school.

Why Chi Gong is important

It`s good for your physical mental and spiritual health

In terms of the proven long term physical benefits, these include: an improved posture, an increased capacity of Oxygen/Carbon Dioxide exchange occurring in the lungs, whereby the expansion and contraction of lungs during breathing and breath holds, restores lung capacity (our lifestyle and breathing habits prohibit us from utilizing the entire lung capacity plus modern humans tend to take a lot of shallow breaths). Moreover, the exercises help normalize breathing frequency, bring improvements to the multiple if not all organ systems, generic revitalizing effect on the whole body.

From the psychological and mental perspectives, practicing Chi Gong in a group acts as the motivation, binds people into a group with the same goals and objectives, a community I would say, that is not bent on greed, violence, personal gain, desire to outperform and compete with others, but focuses on self-achievement, personal and social responsibility, group support, forms a common energy field that supports, protects each and every student and creates bonds that surpasses any bonds known to the materialistic man.
